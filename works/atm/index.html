THE IDEA
In May 2025, ATM relaunched its recruitment campaign, entrusting the strategy and visual treatment to the Communication division of AIM Group. I was responsible for the graphic design of the campaign, including its adaptations for OOH, print, and digital formats, as well as the development of the landing page.

The concept celebrates the brand as a trusted institution and highlights what it means to be part of ATM. The treatment, characterized by a soft line and bold brushstrokes, makes the communication clear, recognizable, and memorable.
