THE IDEA
In May 2024, I was selected by World Athletics for the 2024 Content Creator Programme, alongside three other international creators. Each of us developed a project or format on social media to promote the sport and key competitions in the context of the Olympics and World Championships. I created Aeon Agon, an interactive alternate reality story (ARG): a sci-fi virtual treasure hunt with a final prize. The challenge was to modernize ARGs in order to engage a new niche audience with storytelling. Through a more visual and cinematic approach, as well as the development of a strong branding identity, Aeon Agon created a small universe that captured the curiosity and appreciation of the audience.
