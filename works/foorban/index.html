THE IDEA
I produced the visual assets for Foorban’s Summer 2025 OOH campaign, featured across major hubs in Rome and Emilia-Romagna. By blending photography and 3D rendering, I created high-impact imagery that brought the food startup’s summer vision to life in high-traffic urban spaces.

The goal was to create high-impact, fresh, and appetizing visuals that communicate the quality and convenience of Foorban's offerings.

Advertising by Sabrina Bisi.
